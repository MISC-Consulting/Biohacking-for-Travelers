Email Nurture Sequence (post lead magnet)

Day 0
Deliver the Guide + Quick Win

Send the biomarkers PDF. Include one actionable tip they can do today: "Get 10 min of morning sunlight within 30 min of waking. This single habit outperforms any supplement."

Day 2
The Hidden Cost of Jet Lag (Story)

M.K.'s transformation story — the same case study from the LP. "He was losing 2-3 days every trip... until he tried the Arrival Protocol." Ends with: "Want your own? It's free."

Day 4
The Science Behind the Protocol

Huberman + Attia framework. Position BioEdge as the implementation layer. "You've heard the podcasts. Now get the protocol." CTA: Try your first Arrival Protocol free.

Day 7
"Where Are You Headed Next?" (Soft CTA)

Direct question. Reply-to email. "Tell me your next trip and I'll show you what the protocol covers." Drives engagement and qualifying data.

Day 10
Objection Buster: "I've tried wellness apps before"

Address the #1 objection. This isn't meditation or journaling. It's data-driven protocols built by AI for your exact trip. Wearable data screenshots. CTA: Free protocol.

Target CPM $20-35
Target CTR 2-4%
Email Open Rate 45-55%
Email → Free Trial 8-15%
Budget Split 30%

Bottom of Funnel — Conversion

Hot traffic. LP visitors, free trial users, and email subscribers ready to buy.

BOF

Ad Creative

  • UGC-style first-person stories from founders

  • Scarcity: "347 founding member spots remaining"

Destination & Conversion

  • Landing page: index.html#pricing
  • Offer: $19/mo Inner Circle + 30-day guarantee
  • Conversion event: Purchase / StartTrial
  • Pixel: ViewContent + InitiateCheckout + Purchase

Conversion Email Sequence (post free-trial)

Day 0
Your Arrival Protocol Is Ready

Deliver the protocol immediately. Include: "Here's exactly what to do before, during, and after your flight." Make first value delivery instant.

Day 3
How Did It Go? (Feedback Loop)

"How was your trip? Reply to this email with your Oura/WHOOP data and I'll show you what to optimize next." Drives engagement + qualifies for upgrade.

Day 5
Unlock Unlimited Protocols — Founding Price

Value stack email. "One protocol helped. Imagine having one for every trip." Founding member pricing ($19/mo), limited to 500 spots. 30-day guarantee.

Day 7
Last Chance: Founding Member Spots Closing

Urgency + social proof. "217 founders joined this week. 347 founding spots left." Testimonial from M.K. Price goes to $29/mo after founding round.

Target ROAS 3-5x
Target CPA $15-30
Free → Paid 12-20%
Avg Order Value $19/mo
Budget Split 20%
