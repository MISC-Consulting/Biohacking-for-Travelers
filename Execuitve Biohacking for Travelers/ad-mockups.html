Audience Targeting Blueprint

Cold — Interest-Based

Top-of-funnel awareness and lead magnet

  • Interests: Business travel, Entrepreneurship, Biohacking
  • Interests: Oura Ring, WHOOP, Andrew Huberman
  • Interests: Startup founders, YC, TechCrunch
  • Behaviors: Frequent international travelers
  • Job titles: CEO, Founder, Managing Director
  • Age: 28-55 | Income: Top 10%
  • Exclude: Current customers, LP visitors

Warm — Lookalike + Engaged

Mid-funnel solution-aware

  • 1% Lookalike of lead magnet downloaders
  • 1% Lookalike of LP visitors (top 25% time on page)
  • Instagram/FB page engagers (90 days)
  • Video viewers (75%+ watched)
  • Layered with: Frequent travelers interest
  • Exclude: Current customers

Hot — Retarget

Bottom-of-funnel conversion

  • LP visitors who didn't convert (7-30 days)
  • Lead magnet downloaders who didn't sign up
  • Pricing page visitors
  • Cart/form abandoners
  • Email list — unsubscribed or disengaged
  • Frequency cap: 3 impressions/day
