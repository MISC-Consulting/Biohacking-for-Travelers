Stage 4 — Conversion

Paid Membership Signup

User converts to one of 3 tiers. The "Inner Circle" ($19/mo) is the target tier — unlimited protocols, best value perception. Founding Member ($49/mo) is the profit maximizer.

Tier 1
Single Protocol — $9/mo

Entry tier. 1 protocol/month. Designed to convert to Inner Circle.

Tier 2 (Target)
Inner Circle — $19/mo

Unlimited protocols. Best LTV:CAC ratio. Most popular badge.

Tier 3 (Anchor)
Founding Member — $49/mo

1:1 coaching, bloodwork. Makes $19 look like a steal (Hormozi price anchoring).

Guarantee
"Arrive Ready" — 30-Day Refund

Risk reversal. "If you don't notice a measurable difference, full refund."

Post-conversion
Stage 5 — Retention & Referral

Lifetime Value Maximization

Post-conversion mechanics to increase LTV and generate organic growth through referrals.

Onboarding
First Protocol Win

Immediate protocol generation for their next trip. Time-to-value under 5 minutes.

Upsell
Tier Upgrade Trigger

After 3rd protocol, prompt upgrade to Inner Circle (if on Single) or Founding Member.

Referral
Give $10, Get $10

Viral loop. Each referral reduces effective CAC and grows the waitlist organically.

Community
Private Founder Network

Shared protocols, trip reports, biohacking intel. Increases switching cost + engagement.
